The pilot trap.

Every AI engagement starts the same way. A pilot. Six weeks. One team. "Let's prove it works." Six months later, the pilot is a ghost — technically successful, organizationally stuck. We've seen it enough to name the pattern, and the fix isn't more models or better prompts. It's the unglamorous work of data access and change management. Here's what we do instead.
